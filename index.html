About me

I'm a Senior Software Engineer living in Berkshire with over 10 years experience.

Working at Artesian Solutions

Currently a Senior Software Developer at Artesian Solutions. I am part of a scrum team delivering software that helps our customers find relevant news and information about companies.

Previously at RM

I worked at RM Education for over 10 years and worked my way from a Graduate to Senior Analyst Programmer and then progressing to a Technical Architect and then a Senior Software Engineer.

Team leader

Working with a Development Manager I have lead teams of up to 7 software developers spanning 2 continents on various projects of different sizes. This included working with developers from RM's Software house in India RM ESI.

Projects lasting from a few weeks to over a year

I have worked on projects lasting anything from a few weeks to over a year involving just a few people to projects spanning multiple departments.

Senior Software Engineer

I worked behind the scenes on RM Education's RM Unify product and various other websites in RM's Software Operations Team. I worked on anything from helping develop the main software products and writing support tools to running PowerShell scripts to fix broken data. It was a very varied role and priorities changed at any moment.

My main focus was on Microsoft Azure products where I have developed and deployed cloud services that made use of many Microsoft Azure features.

As a Technical Architect

The Technical Architect role involved turning business requirements into technical solutions. I designed systems that supported RM’s business of selling ICT solutions to educational establishments. These were delivered by RM’s software house in India. Working with the project’s development manager I was involved in most stages of the software development lifecycle. I wrote clear and precise design documents that were formally handed over to the software development team via conference calls. I oversaw all development and supported the team by answering questions and providing technical assistance.

Software Development Lifecycle (SDLC)

I have experience of working in both a waterfall and agile development lifecycle making use of automated testing and continuous integration environments. I also have experience of working within teams that adhere to ISO 9001 Quality Management standard and ISO 27001 Information Security standard.

Spare time

In my spare time I enjoy learning about the latest Microsoft .Net capabilities and trying out the latest preview features in Microsoft Azure. To help with this I have written a Microsoft Azure web site using MVC5, Azure SQL Server, Redis cache and other Azure features.

Money Admin POC Azure web site is MVC 5 website for me to try out new technologies and investigate various design patterns within a real problem solving solution.
